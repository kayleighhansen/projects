Site purpose: This site is a weather forcast site for local areas in Idaho. It will be easy to use and cater to younger audiences or people who just want something simple to check the weather in their area. The website will cover weather reports for Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The site will also report on local storms and provide a photo gallery

The domain for this website would be fowlweather.org which costs $12.95 a year

Typography

In this web page we will be using the fonts "Prompt" and "Lato"

Prompt Title

I like this font because it is not very serious or professional, just like the purpose of my site. I chose a sans-serif font that can be bolded really well and matches the style of the logo.

Lato Text

This font compliments the title well and looks nice in paragraphs. It is easy to read for the children in the target audience and the italisized version looks very artistic.
