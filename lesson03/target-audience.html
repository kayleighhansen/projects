Site purpose: This site is a weather forcast site for local areas in Idaho. It will be easy to use and cater to younger audiences or people who just want something simple to check the weather in their area. The website will cover weather reports for Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The site will also report on local storms and provide a photo gallery

The domain for this website would be fowlweather.org which costs $12.95 a year

Target Audience

This web page will be catered to people who are looking for casual weather updates

Scenario 1: Mom looking for the weather before kids go to school

Scenario 2: Child looking for weather forcast before getting dressed

Scenario 3: Grandma looking for local weather before a vacation

Scenario 4: Young girl looking to see if her game will rain out
