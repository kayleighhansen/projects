Site purpose: This site is a weather forcast site for local areas in Idaho. It will be easy to use and cater to younger audiences or people who just want something simple to check the weather in their area. The website will cover weather reports for Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The site will also report on local storms and provide a photo gallery

The domain for this website would be fowlweather.org which costs $12.95 a year

Target Audience

This web page will be catered to people who are looking for casual weather updates

Wendy, 35 years old, 4 young children with 2 in elementary school. Her kids are very active in sports and she is on all of the PTA's. She checks the weather every morning before her kids wake up. She doesn't like watching the news or big weather stations because she doesn't like hearing about all the disasters around the world. She has enough to worry about and likes to keep things simple.

Jeremy, 10 years old. Jeremy is good at using the computer but his parents have block on a lot of media to make sure he is safe. He likes animals and reading and has a lot of outdoor activities. His parents expect him to responsible and check the weather so he knows if his activites will be canceled. He brings his own jacket to school if he sees it is going to be cold.

Scenario 1: How warm should I dress my kids for school today?

Scenario 2: Is it cold enough to bring a winter coat on my week at grandma's house?

Scenario 3: Will there be rain at my soccer game tomorrow?

Scenario 4: Will school be delayed tomorrow for the snow storm?

Scenario 5: Is the weather app on my phone correct for my area?
