Typography

h1, h2 - Bubblegum Sans

p - Open Sans Light

I like these fonts with the colors and also keeps the light feel of the site. The thicker font for the headers compliments the thin open sans text. I wanted a unique font that would stand out and look best in black against brighter colors. I like how the open sans collapses easily and can be read in small fonts.
