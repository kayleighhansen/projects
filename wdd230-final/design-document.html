Design Document - Scoots Rental Scooters

Site Purpose

Appeal to younger audiences traveling to or around Cancun to show the simplicity of renting scooters and having a big adventure on their vacation.

Target Audience

I am targeting the people that are usually in charge of the family/group vacations to Cancun, which would be a person who is meticulously organized and maybe feeling a bit overwhelmed.

Parents

Parents want something that looks safe but fun for many ages of children, so this website will be colorful and organized but also include a safety section on the rental information and a safety agreement in the reservations page.

Party Organizers

People in charge of parties such as bachelorette parties or friend getaways will be looking for something exhilerating but inexpensive. I will highlight the low prices of Scoots rentals and inslude a link to other fun things to do in the area on the tours page.

Romantic Getaway

Two people on a honeymoon or romantic getaway want to be close to their significant other and also be able to see beautiful scenery. I will include romantic destinations around the area on the tours page.

World Traveler

Some people are basically professionals at seeing unique things around the world and learning about new cultures. For people who are looking to educate themselves, I will incldue historically and culturally significant landmarks in Cancun that would interest the academic traveler.
